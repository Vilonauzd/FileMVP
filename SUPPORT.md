# FileMVP Support

Thank you for using **FileMVP**.

FileMVP is a lightweight utility designed to let users create and rename files with full control over file names and extensions. The app works entirely on-device and does not require an account or internet connection.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does FileMVP collect any data?
No.  
FileMVP does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All files are created and managed locally on your device.

### Does FileMVP require an account or sign-in?
No.  
All functionality is available immediately after installing the app.

### Why canâ€™t I see my files outside the app?
FileMVP operates within iOS file sandboxing rules. Files created in the app remain accessible through supported iOS file locations.

### Does FileMVP support cloud sync?
No.  
FileMVP is intentionally designed to operate locally only.
